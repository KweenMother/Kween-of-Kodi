‚öôÔ∏è Max Threads Explained

What it is: Number of simultaneous scrapers allowed.
Why "2" on Xbox?
- Xbox's 1.75GHz CPU can't handle more without freezing.
- Prevents MemoryError crashes (common with 4+ threads).
